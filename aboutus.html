ABOUT US

Established with a vision to nurture young minds and prepare them for the challenges of the future, Kasiglahan
Village Senior High is a center of learning where students embark on a journey of
discovery, exploration, and growth. Our school is located in the heart of
Kasiglahan Village, where the rich tapestry of culture and
community spirit inspires our educational philosophy.

MISSION

Inspiring Excellence, Building Leaders

Our mission is to provide a dynamic
and engaging educational experience that
equips students with the knowledge, skills,
and values essential for success in an
ever-evolving world. We strive to foster a
love for learning, instill a sense of responsibility, and
empower our students to become compassionate,
informed, and proactive members of society.

VISION

At Kasiglahan Village Senior High School,
our vision is to be a renowned institution
that empowers minds, nurtures talents, and
cultivates character. We aspire to create
an educational environment where every
student is inspired to reach their full potential,
equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values
to excel in a dynamic world.
